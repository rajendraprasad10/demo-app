How to Apply (Commands)

cd /local-manifest-files


Step 1: Apply All Resources

	kubectl apply -f dev-postgres.yml

Step 2: Verify Resources
	kubectl get all -n dev
	kubectl get pvc -n dev
	kubectl get pv


Step 3: Check Pod Logs
	kubectl logs postgres-0 -n dev

Step 4: Exec into Pod
	kubectl exec -it postgres-0 -n dev -- bash

Step 5: Connect to DB
	psql -U postgres -d devopsdb

9. How App Connects to PostgreSQL
	Inside Cluster:
	DB_HOST = "postgres-service"
	DB_PORT = 5432
